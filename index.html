New York

I was alone.

I had a camera.

I walked around the city aimlessly for a week.

I gravitated toward the small moments, the short interactions, the trivial decisions that make up a New Yorker's life.

A man walks home from work. A woman contemplates the quality of an apple.

The result was a series of photos that coincidently make sense together.
